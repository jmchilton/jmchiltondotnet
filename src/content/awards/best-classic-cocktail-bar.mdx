## The Winner: Tina's

[Tina's](https://tinaspgh.com/) takes the crown for best classic cocktail bar in Pittsburgh. Located at 4144 Main Street in Bloomfield, it's a place that truly understands what makes a great cocktail bar.

## Why Tina's Wins

Many bars in Pittsburgh have amazing cocktail selections, but Tina's stands apart with a menu that highlights the classics like no other. Their 50+ classic cocktail menu isn't trying to reinvent the wheel - instead, they focus on representing the culture and history behind these recipes.

### Curation & Execution

The curation is impeccable, but what really sets Tina's apart is the execution. Every drink is made with care and precision.

### The Atmosphere

The bar is among the most romantic and sexy spots in the city. The modern retro vibe creates an atmosphere that has to be experienced to be understood.

### The Staff

Incredibly friendly and engaging. Named after the owner's mother - a woman who made everyone feel at home - that spirit carries through in the service.
