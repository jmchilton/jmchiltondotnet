## Squid Ink Basil Gimlet

**Ingredients:** Gin 2 oz, Lime Juice 0.5 oz, Basil Syrup 0.5 oz, Squid Ink 1/8 tsp
**Instructions:** Shake with ice, strain into coupe glass.
**Garnish:** Basil leaf

---

## Blood Moon Margarita

**Ingredients:** Tequila 2 oz, Blood Orange Juice 1 oz, Lime Juice 0.5 oz, Pomegranate Juice 0.5 oz
**Instructions:** Shake with ice, strain into rocks glass over ice.
**Garnish:** Lime wheel

---

## Crimson Bloom Margarita

**Ingredients:** Tequila 2 oz, Blood Orange Juice 1 oz, Lime Juice 0.5 oz, Hibiscus Syrup 0.5 oz
**Instructions:** Shake with ice, strain into rocks glass over ice.
**Garnish:** Lime wheel

---

## Spiced Chocolate Old Fashioned

**Ingredients:** Bourbon or Rye 2 oz, Demerara Syrup 0.5 oz, Chocolate Bitters 2 dashes, Allspice Bitters 1 dash
**Instructions:** Stir with ice, strain over large ice cube in rocks glass.
**Garnish:** Lemon twist

---

## Corpse Reviver No. 2

**Ingredients:** Gin 2 oz, Bauchant 1 oz, Dry Vermouth 1 oz, Lemon Juice 0.5 oz, Absinthe rinse 1 tsp (optional)
**Instructions:** Shake with ice, strain into chilled coupe.

---

## Crimson Skull Daiquiri

**Ingredients:** Aged Rum 2 oz, Lime Juice 0.5 oz, Hibiscus Syrup 1 oz
**Instructions:** Shake with ice, strain into coupe glass.
**Garnish:** Lime wheel

---

## Haunted Reef Daiquiri _(optional Fernet floater)_

**Ingredients:** Aged Rum 1 oz, Lime Juice 0.5 oz, Demerara Syrup 0.5 oz, Optional Fernet 0.5 oz
**Instructions:** Shake rum, lime, syrup with ice, strain over coupe/rocks glass. Add Fernet float if desired.
**Garnish:** Lime wheel
