## Halloween Themed Drinks

Drinks we've prepared for, should have the right glasses and garnishes and such.

### Black Widow

![](<https://www.thespruceeats.com/thmb/yXs5Updd5RgIzriaNrhi9Cf0itc=/960x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/black-widow-recipe-761008-hero-01-070706a180d04aec9b6736fa1d1f3c19.jpg>)

- https://www.thespruceeats.com/black-widow-recipe-761008
- Make black vodka https://www.thespruceeats.com/how-to-make-black-vodka-760327.
  - 1 bottle of Vodka + 10 drops of blue FC, 10 drops of red FC, 8 drops of green FC.
- Pour 3 oz of cranberry in a tall glass with ice, followed by a float of black vodka.
- Or as shots - half and half.

### Poison Apple Cider

![](https://hips.hearstapps.com/hmg-prod.s3.amazonaws.com/images/poison-apple-cocktail-horizontal-1539985573.jpg?crop=0.874xw:0.952xh;0.126xw,0&resize=768:*)

- https://www.delish.com/cooking/recipe-ideas/a23878264/poison-apple-cocktails-recipe/
- 2.5 oz apple cider, 2.5 oz cranberry, 2oz fireball (sub out Wiggle Cinnamon), 1 oz grenadine, 1/4 tsp gold glitter.

### Freight Night in the Grove

![](<https://www.thespruceeats.com/thmb/aQhPq8VTIl51UvhNri267Q9Mu88=/960x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/fright-night-in-the-grove-cocktail-760774-hero-01-79c6ebd2ba954db1955b5dab2dce9a8d.jpg>)

- https://www.thespruceeats.com/fright-night-in-the-grove-cocktail-760774
- 1/2 reposado Tequila, 2 oz Jaegermeister, 1 oz grapefruit juice, 1/2 simple syrup
- Shake and add to old fashioned glass with ice.
- Garnish with with grapefruit and salt

### Skeleton Key

![](<https://www.thespruceeats.com/thmb/JuG6SRpYYFWlow6vRo1WSRBGIb4=/960x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/skeleton-key-cocktail-recipe-761383-hero-02-5cdcd93be5d2413f9d3a7a8056964c6e.jpg>)

- https://www.thespruceeats.com/skeleton-key-cocktail-recipe-761383
- 1.25 oz bourbon, 3/4 oz of elderflower, 1/2 oz of lemon juice.
- Shake and add to tall glass with ice.
- Top with 3-5 oz of ginger beer.
- Garnish with 8 drops of bitters for effect.

### Ghostbusters

![](<https://www.thespruceeats.com/thmb/UYJ1HXOAGANvxh9uQDuMo-yaIe0=/960x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/ghostbuster-cocktail-recipe-759668-hero-01-5264544fa57d4d9c8b331c1638e4d8fc.jpg>)

- https://www.thespruceeats.com/ghostbuster-cocktail-recipe-759668
- 1:1 peach schnapps and Midori - mix and add to martini glass. Add a few drops of Irish cream.

### Jack O' Lantern

![](<https://www.thespruceeats.com/thmb/7zNx6WSOOlu09kzljL3d5iyGPR8=/960x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/jackolantern-level-example-6e53b034385543bf86de2a24984a4c26.jpg>)

- https://www.thespruceeats.com/jack-o-lantern-cocktail-recipe-759441
- 1.5 oz cognac, .5 oz Grand Marnier, 1 oz orange juice, 2-3 oz ginger ale.
- Pour into glass with ice.
- Garnish is important - take orange cross section and small lime twist to make a pumpkin topper.

### Vampire's Kiss Martini

![](<https://www.thespruceeats.com/thmb/dn8sYIUWeDaRCZOCBEQ-vfQyDMs=/960x0/filters:no_upscale():max_bytes(150000):strip_icc():format(webp)/vampire-kiss-martini-recipe-761200-hero-01-062830abd98c470db5e4bc5fe327d3c3.jpg>)

- https://www.thespruceeats.com/vampire-kiss-martini-recipe-761200
- Rim glass with raspberry liquor and red sugar.
- 3/4 oz raspberry liquor, 1.5 oz vodka, 1.5 oz sparkling wine.
- Mix half liquor in glass with vodka. Top with sparkling.
- Pour other liquor over bar spoon to float it

### Corpse Reviver

![](https://www.acouplecooks.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/Corpse-Reviver-No-2-001.jpg)

- https://www.acouplecooks.com/corpse-reviver/
- 1 oz gin, 1 oz lemon juice, 1 oz Cointreau (or Grand Marnier), 1 oz Lillet Blanc (sub out dry vermouth), 1/2 teaspoon absinthe
- Shake til cold,
- Strain into coup
- Garnish with orange wedge or peel.
