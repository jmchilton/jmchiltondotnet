## Vicious Mockery

- ¼ Orange, Muddled
- 1 ½ oz. Spiced Rum
- 1 oz. Jaegermeister
- Several dashes Peychaud's Bitters, to taste
- Orange Twist for garnish

## Turn Undead

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/706ec49818376612d2f16ffdb8312124/tumblr_inline_pg73sjJJfs1qdq19t_1280.jpg)

- ½ oz Elderflower Liqueur
- 2 oz White Rum
- 2 oz White Cranberry Juice
- ¼ oz Lime Juice
- 4 Basil Leaves
- Club Soda

## Druidcraft

![](https://64.media.tumblr.com/ad135f3e40d7322659ded2d693e7b861/tumblr_inline_pg1yo5fvwT1qdq19t_1280.jpg)

- 1½ oz Gin
- 1 oz Elderflower liqueur
- ½ oz Honey
- ¾ oz Lemon Juice
- 2 oz Club Soda
- 1 tsp Culinary Lavender
- 3 Sage Leaves

## Hunter's Mark

![](https://www.mainespirits.com/sites/default/files/styles/recipe_-_large_image_style/public/makers-mark-penicillin-cocktaill-1200x1600.jpg?itok=3AdW26Cd)

(A play on penicilian with Maker's Mark)

- 2 Parts Maker's Mark Bourbon
- ½ Scotch
- 1 oz Fresh Lemon Juice
- ½ oz Honey Syrup
- 1 disk Fresh Ginger
- Candied Ginger
